Game Description and Rules

You will be presented with 5 questions randomly. You will have 10 seconds to answer each question.
The feedback will provided to you directly when you select the answer. You can only choose once. You CANNOT skip any questions during the game.
You will pass the text when you score 60 or more.
You can choose to store you score in the application and also be able to access the Top 5 Scores or search for your own score.
Good luck!
